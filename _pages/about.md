I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT) in Genova, Italy, working on elephant-inspired soft robotic manipulators and monolithic systems.

My current work focuses on integrated design—combining morphology, sensing, and control—to build capable, robust soft manipulators.
